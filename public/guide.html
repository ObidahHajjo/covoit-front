to use Covoit.

Find a ride, offer a trip, coordinate with chat, and manage your account — step by step.

🔑

Step 1

Getting Started

1

Create your account

Open the app and tap Create your route. Enter your email address and choose a strong password (at least 8 characters with uppercase, lowercase, number, and special character).

2

Complete your profile

After registration, you will be prompted to complete your profile — add your first name, pseudo, and your vehicle details. This unlocks all features of the app.

3

Sign in to your account

Already have an account? Tap Sign in, enter your email and password, and you will be taken directly to your dashboard.

4

Forgot your password?

On the sign in page, tap Forgot password. Enter your email and a reset link will be sent to your inbox. Follow the link to set a new password.

💡

Your session is remembered securely. You will not need to sign in again unless you explicitly sign out.

🔍

Step 2

Find a Trip

1

Open the search page

From the bottom navigation, tap Find. The search form lets you enter your departure city, arrival city, and travel date.

2

Enter your route

Start typing a city name — an autocomplete list will appear. Select your departure and arrival cities. Choose your desired travel date from the date picker.

3

Browse results

Available trips are listed with driver name, departure time, available seats, and price. Use the pagination controls to browse more results.

4

View trip details

Tap any result to open the full trip details — departure and arrival addresses, vehicle information, driver contact options, and seat availability.

💡

If no trips match your search, try adjusting the date or check back later — drivers publish new trips regularly.

📋

Step 3

Book a Trip

1

Contact the driver

From the trip details page, you can contact the driver via in-app chat or email to confirm your seat and discuss pickup details before booking.

2

Confirm your booking

Once you are ready, tap the booking button on the trip details page. Your booking is registered and visible under Bookings in the navigation.

3

Track your booking

Open Bookings from the navigation bar to see all your upcoming and past reservations. Tap a booking to view full details and trip status.

💬

Chat with driver

Coordinate pickup point, time, and any last-minute changes directly in the chat.

📧

Email option

Prefer email? Use the email contact option on the trip details page for a formal message.

🗂️

Booking history

All past and upcoming bookings are stored in your Bookings section with full details.

🚗

For Drivers

Offer a Trip

1

Add your vehicle

Before publishing a trip, make sure your vehicle is registered in Account → Car. You need to select a brand, model, and year so passengers can identify your vehicle.

2

Publish a new trip

Go to My Trips and tap New Trip. Fill in the departure city, arrival city, departure date and time, number of available seats, and price per seat.

3

Manage your trips

All your published trips appear in My Trips. Tap any trip to see passenger bookings, manage seats, and access contact options for each passenger.

4

Contact your passengers

From a trip's detail page, you can chat with or email each passenger individually to coordinate pickup points, timing, or any change before departure.

💡

Publish your trip at least a few hours before departure so passengers have enough time to find and contact you.

💬

Coordination

Chat & Messages

1

Open your inbox

Tap Chat in the bottom navigation to see all your active conversations — both as a driver and as a passenger.

2

Real-time messaging

Messages are delivered in real-time. The app uses WebSocket technology for instant delivery. If the connection is unavailable, the inbox polls automatically every few seconds.

3

Unread notifications

A badge on the Chat icon shows the number of unread messages. A toast notification appears at the bottom of the screen when a new message arrives.

💡

Use chat to confirm exact pickup address, any change in departure time, or luggage details — all in one thread per trip.

👤

Settings

Your Account

✏️

Edit profile

Update your first name, pseudo, and personal information from the Account section.

🚗

Manage your car

Add or update your vehicle brand, model, and year. Required to publish trips as a driver.

🌍

Language

Switch the app language between English, French, and Arabic at any time from any screen.

🔒

Password & security

Change your password via the forgot password flow — a reset link is sent to your email.

💡

Keep your profile complete and your vehicle up to date — passengers and drivers see this information before confirming a trip.
